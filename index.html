Our Story

Where the ocean meets the table.

Memorial is inspired by Newfoundland's maritime heritage. Every plate draws from the island's coastal foraging, crisp sea air, and a reverence for local artisans. Our kitchen celebrates the Atlantic's depth — from wild-caught seafood to meadow-grown herbs and berries.

Designed as a modern tribute to St. John's, the space blends warmth, light, and sea-swept textures for a refined yet welcoming experience.
